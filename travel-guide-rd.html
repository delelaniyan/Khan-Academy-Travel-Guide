Canada

Canada is the second best country to live in, in the whole world! Why not visit it if you get the chance. Visit places like Montreal, and visit the biodome, or 'La ronde'. Or go to Toronto and watch the Blue Jays baseball team play, from the top of the CN Tower. Then, drive a few hours over the Canada's Capital, and visit the Parliment Hill where Justin Trudeau is making big decisions.
